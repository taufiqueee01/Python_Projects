INTERVIEW MCQ SYSTEM - HOW TO USE
=================================

WHAT IT DOES:
Practice timed interview questions to boost confidence before real interviews.

HOW TO RUN:
1. Open terminal and go to this folder
2. Run: python main.py
3. Select option 1: Start Quiz
4. Answer 10 questions in 3 minutes
5. View score and performance rating

YOU GET:
- Score out of 10
- Percentage
- Performance rating (Excellent, Good, etc)
- High score tracking

Data saves automatically in high_scores.txt
