STUDY SESSION ANALYZER - HOW TO USE
===================================

WHAT IT DOES:
Track study sessions and see your productivity patterns.

HOW TO RUN:
1. Open terminal and go to this folder
2. Run: python main.py
3. Choose option from menu
4. Follow prompts

BASIC WORKFLOW:
- Add Study Session: Enter subject and hours
- View Total Hours: See total study time
- Weekly Summary: Check last 7 days activity

Data saves automatically in study_data.csv
