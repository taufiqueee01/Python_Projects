RESUME ANALYZER - HOW TO USE
============================

WHAT IT DOES:
Check your resume quality and get a score out of 100 with improvement suggestions.

HOW TO RUN:
1. Open terminal and go to this folder
2. Run: python main.py
3. Select option 1: Analyze Resume
4. Paste your resume text
5. Press Enter twice when done

YOU GET:
- Score out of 100
- List of keywords found
- Missing keywords to add
- Improvement suggestions

No data file needed - analysis is instant!
